# Welcome to RJDevDen 👋

_"My creations are an expression of my love for gaming, automation, and scripting. I'm glad to have you here!"_

My ultimate goal is to help you save time on mundane and repeatable tasks so you can spend it on the activities that actually matter—like playing the games you love. I firmly believe that **"no job is too small to be automated if you have to do it more than once."** I love learning new tools and techniques to automate various activities, and this GitHub organization is where I share those solutions with you.

## 🛠️ What I Do

While my current focus is heavily rooted in the game modding scene, my passion for automation extends to anything that can be made faster and more efficient through coding/scripting.

**Game Modding Tools:** Creating robust applications and scripts to automate tedious modding tasks (like BA2 archives merging or ESLification of plugins).

**Shared Frameworks:** Building centralized libraries (like RJLib) to help other modders and tools work together seamlessly.

**Educational Content:** Producing YouTube guides and tutorials to help you understand my tools and general game modding concepts.

**Future Automation:** Always exploring new ways to automate outside of gaming—if there's a repetitive digital task, I'm probably trying to script it! 😉

## 📂 Featured Projects

* [**BMAT (BA2 Merging Automation Tool):**](https://github.com/RJDevDen/BMAT) The BMAT (BA2 Merging Automation Tool) tool designed to automate the lengthy and complex process of merging BA2 archives and loose folders for Fallout 4 mods.

* [**BMAT_CC (BA2 Merging Automation Tool) for CC (Creation Club) Mods:**](https://github.com/RJDevDen/BMAT) The BMAT_CC same as BMAT is an automation tool for BA2 merging but specialising in Creation Club mods.

* [**ACE (Automated Compaction & ESLification):**](https://github.com/RJDevDen/ACE) A powerful tool designed to automate the complex and lengthy process of compacting and ESLifying plugins.

* **RJLib:** A centralized framework and shared script library mandatory for my tools, built for reusability and efficiency.
